Daily Tasks

Step 1

The first thing to do when you arrive is to check if there are new orders online to set the ball rolling.

Check the online orders
Step 2

The next thing to do is to check if there are enough stock before you start working on the order.

Check the stock
Step 3

This is the time to check the newly arrive stock and ensure they are recorded on the system.

Sort out incoming Stock
Step 4

At this stage, you will need to verify customers' payment record before processing the order.

Check payment made by customers
Step 5

Package the orders and ensure that you do a quality check for the items and delivery address.

Package orders for delivery
Step 6

Process the delivery documents online for delivery company.

Make arrangement for all order collection by delivery company
Step 7

This is the time to record all the sales made in shop, online and document correctly.

Record the sales for the day
Step 8

Is there enough stock to last the next two days? if not, place an order.

How you can work with us

Contact Our Office
Our office is opened between 9am and 5pm except for weekends and bank holidays.

Locate Our Contact Form
You only need to complete the form and our HR will be in touch within 72hours.

You can send us your resume
Our HR will review your resume and get back to you within two weeks.

Our Two-stage Interview Process
Our HR will contact you upon reviewing your resume. If you are successful in the first stage, you will be invited to our for the second stage.

Food Hygiene Certification
You will be expected to complete the food hygiene course for certification if your interview is successful.

Visit Us
You can contact our central office by arranging an appointment with the HR. Contact Central Office
